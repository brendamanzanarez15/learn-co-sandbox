About Amy Hennig

Let's find out about Amy Hennig!

Name - Amy Hennig
Date of Birth - August 19, 1964
Current Age - 53


Amy Hennig has worked in the video game since the late 1980's.
Most of her early jobs had Nintendo games involved.
She was employed as an animator and artist.
She feels that once game writers focus on creative
expressions, video games will greatly improve and focusing
too much on graphics can inhibit a game.
Amy likes to use supporting characters to highlight
personality aspects through interactions within the script.
Amy Hennig worked on the Uncharted series on the
Play Station 3 making the population stare speachless at
her work with unbelievable graphics.

Amy Hennig was a part of the staff that created the Uncharted series.
The series didnt recieve much attention due to The Last Of Us
and Left Behind. The staff working on Uncharted was very small compared to
the others and wasn't expected to have a great success but became very popular
after the release date. the staff working on Uncharted didn't recieve the
resources needed to survive. Some found the situation understandable but
